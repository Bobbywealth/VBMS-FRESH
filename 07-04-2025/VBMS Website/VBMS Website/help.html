Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with VBMS?

Welcome to VBMS! Here's how to get started:

  1. Complete the onboarding wizard (if you haven't already)
  2. Set up your business profile in Settings
  3. Connect your integrations (POS, phone system, etc.)
  4. Explore your dashboard to familiarize yourself with features
  5. Set up your first tasks and inventory items

Need help? Visit the Support Center to create a ticket.

What is the onboarding wizard and do I need to complete it?

The onboarding wizard is a 5-step setup process that helps configure your VBMS system:

  • Step 1: Business Information
  • Step 2: Team Setup
  • Step 3: Feature Selection
  • Step 4: Phone Integration
  • Step 5: POS Integration

While not mandatory, completing it ensures your system is properly configured for your business needs.

How do I track my orders?

You can track orders in several ways:

  • Dashboard: View recent orders on your main dashboard
  • Orders Page: Access detailed order management from the sidebar
  • Search: Use the search function to find specific orders
  • Filters: Filter by status, date, customer, or amount

Each order shows real-time status updates and delivery tracking information.

Can I modify or cancel an order?

Order modifications depend on the current status:

  • Pending Orders: Can be modified or cancelled
  • Processing Orders: Limited modifications possible
  • Shipped Orders: Contact support for assistance
  • Delivered Orders: Cannot be modified

To modify an order, go to Orders page, find your order, and click "Edit" if available.

How do I manage my inventory?

VBMS provides comprehensive inventory management:

  • Add Items: Use the "Add Item" button to create new inventory
  • Track Stock: Monitor quantity levels and set low-stock alerts
  • Categories: Organize items by category for easy management
  • Suppliers: Track supplier information and reorder points
  • Reports: Generate inventory reports and analytics
How do I set up low stock alerts?

To set up low stock alerts:

  1. Go to your Inventory page
  2. Click on an item to edit it
  3. Set the "Minimum Stock Level" field
  4. Enable "Low Stock Alerts" in your Settings
  5. Choose notification preferences (email, dashboard, etc.)

You'll receive notifications when items fall below the minimum level.

How does the camera monitoring work?

VBMS camera monitoring provides:

  • Live Feeds: Real-time camera feeds from your locations
  • Recording: Automatic recording and storage
  • Motion Detection: Alerts when motion is detected
  • Remote Access: View cameras from anywhere
  • Mobile Support: Access via mobile devices

Contact support to set up camera integrations for your business.

How do I create and assign tasks?

Task management in VBMS:

  1. Go to the Tasks page from your sidebar
  2. Click "New Task" to create a task
  3. Fill in task details (title, description, due date)
  4. Assign to team members
  5. Set priority level
  6. Track progress through completion

Tasks can be organized using the Kanban board view for better workflow management.

Can I set recurring tasks?

Yes! VBMS supports recurring tasks:

  • Daily: Tasks that repeat every day
  • Weekly: Tasks that repeat weekly
  • Monthly: Tasks that repeat monthly
  • Custom: Set custom recurrence patterns

When creating a task, enable "Recurring" and select your preferred schedule.

How do I update my profile and business information?

To update your information:

  1. Go to Settings from your sidebar
  2. Click on the "Profile" tab
  3. Update your personal information
  4. Go to "Business" tab for business details
  5. Upload logo and update business hours
  6. Save your changes

Changes are saved automatically and take effect immediately.

How do I change my password?

To change your password:

  1. Go to Settings → Security tab
  2. Click "Change Password"
  3. Enter your current password
  4. Enter your new password (must meet security requirements)
  5. Confirm your new password
  6. Click "Update Password"

For security, you'll be logged out and need to sign in with your new password.

How does billing work?

VBMS billing information:

  • Plans: Choose from Basic, Pro, or Enterprise plans
  • Billing Cycle: Monthly or annual billing available
  • Payment Methods: Credit card, ACH, or invoice
  • Invoices: Automatic invoicing with detailed breakdowns
  • Usage: Track feature usage and overages

View billing details in Settings → Billing tab.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes! Plan changes are easy:

  • Upgrades: Take effect immediately with prorated billing
  • Downgrades: Take effect at next billing cycle
  • Features: Access to new features is instant on upgrade
  • Data: All your data is preserved during plan changes

Contact support if you need assistance with plan changes.

I'm having trouble logging in. What should I do?

Login troubleshooting steps:

  1. Check credentials: Verify email and password
  2. Reset password: Use "Forgot Password" if needed
  3. Clear cache: Clear browser cache and cookies
  4. Try different browser: Test with another browser
  5. Check internet: Ensure stable internet connection

If issues persist, contact support with your email address.

Why are my integrations not working?

Integration troubleshooting:

  • Check connections: Verify all integrations are properly connected
  • API keys: Ensure API keys are valid and not expired
  • Permissions: Check that VBMS has necessary permissions
  • Network: Verify firewall settings allow connections
  • Updates: Check if third-party services have updates

Go to Settings → Integrations to check connection status.

The system seems slow. How can I improve performance?

Performance optimization tips:

  • Browser: Use Chrome or Firefox for best performance
  • Cache: Clear browser cache regularly
  • Extensions: Disable unnecessary browser extensions
  • Internet: Ensure stable, high-speed internet connection
  • Data: Archive old data to improve load times

Contact support if performance issues continue.
